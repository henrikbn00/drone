Steps in what they do

call for help

The emergency center gets the call for help to an emergency and all the drones are ready to takeoff. Which drone that fits best for the mission the team who gets the mission decide.

On the way to the scene

The drone team consist of two people. The one who is controlling the drone, and the other person who is instructing the controller and is responsible of gathering information of the scene to pass on to the rescue team that is on its way.

On the scene

When the drone is at the scene the team should act quick to get a view over the situation. They should see if there are any threats or dangers to the rescue team that is on its way. They should try and clarify who is most damaged and need help immediately. Get the equipment they transported to someone useful.

The rescue team arrives

From the point the ambulance or other rescue units arrive the scene, the emmergency drones stand down having done what they could and giving all the information to the rescue teams.

After the emergency

The teams review the operation and footage, try and learn from the good things they did, and from the things they could have done better.
