The emergency station

The flying medic is stationed at the emergency station. At the emergency station the drones are prepared and ready for an emergency call. This is the place where the team that controls the drones will operate and work. For each drone there will be at least to persons that cooperate in the tasks that will be done under an emergency mission. One person controls the drone while the other persons task is to navigate other person and gather important information that will be given to the rescue teams that will be reaching the emergency scene. In the emergency station there will be a variety of drones ready for a mission that fits the designed drones. The drones have different strength and weaknesses and its up to the team to choose which drone to use at any given time. Emergency drones will be crucial in the future in the mission to save life and use every second effectively.

How the emergency drones operate

Call for help

The emergency center gets the call for help to an emergency, and all the drones are ready to takeoff. Which drone that fits best for the mission the team who gets the mission decide.

On the way to the scene

The drone team consist of two people. The one who is controlling the drone, and the other person who is instructing the controller and is responsible of gathering information of the emergency to pass on to the rescue team that is on its way.

On the scene

When the drone is at the scene the team should act quick to get a view over the situation. They should see if there are any threats or dangers to the rescue team that is on its way. They should try and clarify who is most damaged and need help immediately. The drone should aldo get the equipment they transported to someone useful.

The rescue team arrives

From the point the ambulance or other rescue units arrive the scene, the flying medic stand down having done what they could and having given all the information to the rescue teams.

After the emergency

The teams review the operation and footage, try and learn from the good things they did, and from the things they could have done better.
