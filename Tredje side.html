Repair Station

The emergency drones idea also include a repair station where people can come and deliver drones for the trained and professional staff to repair. THe repair station is in the same building where the teams operate in an eventual emergency calling and if there is an emmergency they get into action in no time. The repairs shop gives the facility money to use on the drones for maintence and other investment. The priority is always the emergency department and all customers knows that if an emergency happens, the staff must prioritize the emergency call.
