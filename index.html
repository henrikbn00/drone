Emergency drones and Repair station

Emergency drones are drones that will be the first helping hand to reach an emergency site and help the people in need. When an emergency occurs, every second is crucial and can be the difference between life and death. It is important to use every technology and equipment available to save lives. Therefore, we represent the idea emergency drones. The emergency drones are drones designed with the purpose of saving life and give crucial information that is needed in an emergency.

Why can emergency drones be helpful?

  1. They reach destinations quicker
  2. They reach areas that can be difficult to reach by more normal transport methods
  3. The drones give much needed information before rescue teams reach the site
  4. The drones can deliver needed equipment to the emergency places quick
  5. Have nightvision and thermographic camera if needed.
